## Business Context

### Current Lead Strategy: The Working Founder Podcast

RM Marketing runs "The Working Founder" podcast as their primary lead generation engine:

1. **Prospecting**: Scrape social media platforms for influencers with 100K+ followers who have poorly monetized presences
2. **Outreach**: Cold email inviting them to be podcast guests
3. **Current Performance**: 34% response rate on invitations
4. **Open Question**: How to transition podcast guests into sales conversations about monetization services

### Core Offer

**$100K Audience Monetization System (AMS) Build** — A done-for-you service that builds automated revenue infrastructure for high-engagement influencers.
