# Scope of Work

## Engagement Overview

**Project:** Sales Department Buildout
**Client:** RM Marketing / GHL Mastery
**Consultants:** Chrix (Raise the Vibe) & Sarah
**Duration:** 90 days (with optional ongoing support)
**Investment:** ~$80,000

---

## Scope Summary

This engagement will design, build, and install a complete sales infrastructure for RM Marketing, enabling them to scale from ~$35K/month to $100K+/month. The deliverable is a functioning sales department — people, processes, and technology — that the client can operate independently.

---

## Assumptions

This scope assumes:

1. **Access:** Full access to GHL account, payment processors, ad accounts, and all relevant systems
2. **Data:** Ability to export and analyze all historical data
3. **Availability:** Client stakeholders available for weekly calls and async communication
4. **Decision Authority:** Chrix and Sarah have authority to implement changes within agreed parameters
5. **Budget:** Client has budget for recommended hires (setter, closer) beyond consulting fees
6. **Timeline:** 90-day engagement with possibility of extension
7. **Branding:** Access to brand assets, logos, and style guidelines

---

## Change Management

### Change Request Process

1. Either party identifies need for scope change
2. Written change request submitted
3. Impact assessment (timeline, cost, resources)
4. Discussion and negotiation
5. Written approval from both parties
6. Scope document updated

### Scope Creep Prevention

- Weekly scope check-ins during status calls
- All new requests evaluated against original scope
- Additional work quoted separately
- Clear documentation of all decisions

---

## Acceptance Criteria

Each deliverable will be considered accepted when:

1. Deliverable matches agreed specification
2. Client has reviewed and provided feedback
3. Reasonable revisions have been made
4. Client confirms in writing (email acceptable)

Final engagement completion requires:

1. All deliverables accepted
2. Team members hired and trained
3. Systems operational and documented
4. Handoff training completed
5. 30-day post-handoff support period concluded
